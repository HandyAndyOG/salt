A short engaging pitch of yourself and why you’re applying

I come from a variety of backgrounds and educations, throughout all of which I have excelled, showing that I can adapt and take on steep learning curves. I am a self-taught junior full-stack developer with 1.5 years of experience; however, I require a deeper dive into JavaScript and its frameworks, to have a successful career in Stockholm. I came across </salt> and I immediately knew that this was the right path for me.

Describe when your passion for coding started and why

I was tasked to redo the companies’ website and fix bugs through WordPress. I found trying to fix bugs on the backend of WordPress very limiting and difficult. I decided to learn how to code and recode the entire website from scratch. Throughout the steep learning curves, frustrations, and many hours of research; I found an immense amount of joy and satisfaction. Like as if a spark in my mind was ignited, I was hooked to coding and couldn’t go back to ordinary work tasks. Since building the website, I have in my own spare time enrolled in the Odin-Project and the python institute to further expand my knowledgebase of JavaScript and Python.

Why Salt?

After listening to Marcus’s launch event, I am confident that the above structure is a good fit for me; I thrive in a high intensity work environment, where we are expected to figure out the problems at hand. By giving us faulty code or implementing mob programming, we are forced to understand the intricacies, thus building a solid coding-knowledge foundation. Additionally, the thought of working in a professional team and being placed in work environments provides unmatched learning potential.
